Also are the flag days the Election Day of the Riigikogu or local government, days when referendums are held and the Election Day of the European Parliament. On flag days, government and local authority agencies and legal persons in public law shall hoist the Estonian flag. On Independence Day, Victory Day and the Day of the Restoration of Independence, the Estonian flag shall be hoisted on all residential buildings, commercial buildings and office buildings. The Estonian flag shall be hoisted at sunrise but not later than 8.00 a.m. and shall be lowered at sunset but not later than 10 p.m. The Estonian flag shall not be lowered on Midsummer Night.
